About Us

 A Minecraft Server to house all of AtePlant's adventure maps (+ some others). This server pushes map-making to its limits by providing custom tools to allow creators to make the most creative maps they can dream up. Whether it be difficult parkour, fast-paced ice boat racing, intricate mazes, or convoluted puzzles, The AtePlant Archive strives to provide unique and fun experiences.
