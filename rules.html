Server Rules
Play fair, or else.

These rules apply to all players on the server and in our Discord. Breaking them will result in a permanent ban or permanent mute.

Rules
1
No Cheating
Hacked clients, macros, and any mod that gives an unfair advantage are not allowed. Mods like OptiFine and performance mods are fine.
2
No Hate Speech
Slurs, harassment, and targeted toxicity ain't allowed.
3
Do Not Grief Others
Don't intentionally ruin someone else's run, destroy builds/maps purposefully, or interfere with other's gameplay negatively.
4
Do Not Abuse Exploits
If you find a bug, report it. Using server exploits to produce invalid records or gain unfair advantages will get you banned.
5
Do Not Abuse Map Skips
Major sequence breaks and out-of-bounds skips must be submitted on the Discord. Runs that use them will be scrubbed, and exploiting them without reporting it will result in a ban.
6
No Spam
Keep chat readable. Spamming the same message or flooding chat will get you a mute.
7
Keep It Appropriate
Explicit or sexual content in chat, usernames, or skins is not allowed.
8
Don't Impersonate Staff
Pretending to be a staff member or speaking with false authority will get you banned.
Appeals

Bans are permanent. If you think yours was a mistake, you can appeal in our Discord server. There are no guarantees.
